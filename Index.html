From November 1975 to September 1976, artist Roy Colmer photographed every door he passed while walking the streets of New York. He then organized them sequentially, grouped them by location. From 2022 to 2024, I collected audio clips of conversations with current residents, business owners, and visitors passing in and out of these doors. I did so with the intention of answering these questions: What was behind these doors? What is behind these doors? I've compiled some of Colmer's doors into this new archive where you can group and search for them, not by street, block, and building number, but by key words that describe the stories and memories related to them. The website is relatively small and the search function isn't perfect yet (for example, you cannot search for synonyms or near-matches) but I hope that it nevertheless reveals the potential to expand, reorganize, and reimagine the archive.

Made by Serena Deng in May 2024, for my Archives of Possibility final project.
